Common Problems & Solutions

Common Issues and Their Solutions

If you encounter any difficulties, try using a different browser or computer: In most cases, switching to another browser or computer can resolve the issue.

Out of Memory Error:

If you receive an error message indicating insufficient memory, you may need to close some tabs or applications or restart your computer or browser to free up memory.

Malware:

Malicious software, often accidentally installed, can disrupt your browser by redirecting web requests to ad-laden pages. Be cautious when downloading software from the internet, as unscrupulous individuals may add malware to the installers, damaging your computer. If your system is infected, remove the malware promptly. Microsoft offers a free malware removal tool that you may find helpful.

Plugins/Extensions:

Faulty plugins and extensions can frequently disrupt web functionality and compromise your online security and privacy. Avoid installing questionable plugins or extensions and limit your use to those that are absolutely necessary.

Firewalls/Proxies:

Firewalls and proxies can interfere with web requests, particularly web sockets. If your firewall or proxy is configured to block the normal operation of this site, you will need to reconfigure it. We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause.
